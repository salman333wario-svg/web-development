THE CAT ADVENTURE

THE CURIOUS KITTEN

Once upon a time, a little kitten named Whiskers explored the garden.

MEETING THE DOG

Whiskers met a friendly dog who wanted to play.

THE CHASE

The dog ran around, and Whiskers chased it through the flowers.

SAFE ON THE TREE

Finally, Whiskers climbed a tree to watch from above.

A NEW FRIEND

Whiskers made friends with the dog, and they played happily together.

HOME TIME

As the sun set, Whiskers returned home, tired but joyful.

LESSON LEARNED

Whiskers learned that every new friend can make an adventure exciting!
